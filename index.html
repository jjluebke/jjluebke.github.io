haven

Project type:
Public mobile app, website
Plaform:
iOS and Android
Stack:
AngularJS on Cordova, NodeJS and MongoDB on Heroku
Contributions:
Interaction design, UI design, full stack development as part of a team of 4 full-time developers. Also preformed user testing and conducted user-interviews to improve prouct design and fit.
Challenge:
Develop a product for a unproven market of digital management of home services and the tools for homeowners to take better care of their home

Summary:

Haven provided services to homeowners that helped them to both understand what they need to do to maintain their home as well as making it easy to hire professionals. Initially, consumers used Haven via an installed mobile app, but was later expanded to incorporate a responsive web app and even phone and SMS service. Order tracking was managed using custom, web-based admin tools, Trello and on-staff customer service.

ollier masonry

Project type:
Private mobile app
Plaform:
iOS
Stack:
Javascript/HTML on Cordova, NodeJS and Postgres on Heroku
Contributions:
End-to-end design, development and distribution
Challenge:
Masonry company needs to be able to manage workforce of more than 60 employees at many job sites. Previously, this work was handled by a single individual on paper.

Summary:

The app and service allow for several supervisors to log in to create jobs, assign workers to those jobs, and track resources including time and automated job site reports. Non-privileged employees can view the app, to see when they are scheduled to work, at which job, and who with. The app is distributed in a closed system via the Apple Enterprise program.

Office administrators can log-in to a web-based reporting page, where they can review timesheets for a given job/employee as well as review daily job reports, which serve as a historical record of work preformed and any issues encountered.
